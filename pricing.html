Questions answered

Pricing FAQ

Is early-tester access really free?

Yes. Early testers who install now can use every current feature for $0. Today’s browser extension and Windows app have no trial timer, checkout, subscription, or paid-feature gate.

What do you expect from an early tester?

Nothing formal. Use CodePrettify on real work and send feedback when something breaks, feels slow, or could be clearer. Feedback is welcome, not required.

Are both products free?

Yes. Install the browser extension, Windows app, or both for $0. Their features differ because they support different workflows.

Do I need an account or payment details?

No. Install from the Chrome Web Store, Microsoft Edge Add-ons, or Microsoft Store and start using it.

Does my document leave my device?

Core formatting, validation, conversion, comparison, inspection, and playground processing stay on your device. The HTTP Client makes a network request only after you press Send, to the URL shown in its request composer. Read the privacy policy for the complete details.

Could pricing change later?

The CodePrettify products and features offered here are $0 for early testers who install now. Future optional products, services, or feature tiers may be priced separately, and any change will be communicated clearly.
